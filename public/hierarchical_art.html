Hierarchical ART

Three ART layers at increasing vigilance. Low vigilance → broad categories. High vigilance → fine-grained subtypes. Each episode is independently classified at every level.
Layers

Coarse Level Broad

Loose match required. Forms super-categories.

Mid Level Category

Medium match. Typically separates birds/fish/plants etc.

Fine Level Specific

Strict match. Separates individual concepts.
